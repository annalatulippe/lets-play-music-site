Benefits of joining a
group music program

01. Academic Performance

Proven to improve all around academic performance as well as increase grade average.

02. Cognitive Development

Music education can help to develop discipline, patience, memory, and critical thinking skills as well prevent cognitive decline.

03. Gain Confidence

Let's Play Music & Make Art creates a safe environment where growth, creativity, confidence, and social skills are nurtured while having fun!

Rock Bands

This music-filled class celebrates the unique joys of your growing child. Each week your child will love singing, dancing, and playing instruments with you and her new friends and you’ll love helping her practice a wide variety of abilities such as gross and fine motor skills, turn-taking, social skills, and active listening. In class, we’ll also share tips and ideas for using music throughout the week. Plus, the fun (and learning!) continue with the Kindermusik at-home digital materials, including music from class, musical activities, and each month’s story.
