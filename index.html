Bringing Excellence in Music & Art Education to the Southern NH Community for over 25 Years

Learning music and making art has never been this much fun! Let's Play Music is southern NH's foremost comprehensive music and visual art program with four convenient locations in Derry, Manchester, Salem, and Hooksett New Hampshire.


Our mission is to enrich peoplesâ€™ lives through music and art education, and support creative growth and expression through vibrant community-based programming.


Our family-run program is designed to create the best overall educational experience for people of all ages. We teach all instruments and voice, as well as art classes.
