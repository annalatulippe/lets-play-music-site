Home Music Private Lessons

Our mission is to enrich peoples’ lives through music and art education, and support creative growth and expression through vibrant community-based programming.

Our private lesson program is designed to create the best overall educational experience for people of all ages. We teach private music lessons for all instruments & voice and all skill levels

Whether you're a beginner looking to get started on an instrument, or an advanced player looking to refine you're technique, our music instructors facilitate your strengths while providing a solid foundation and making learning fun!
